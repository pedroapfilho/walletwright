  over "whatever popup is pending", consistent with the existing `approve()` contract. Not a bug.

  the usable-button gate plus the closed-popup retry in `controller.ts`. Not worth further change.

  option): rejected in favor of the 008 drift-guard test; the refactor trades readability for

  painful enough to justify it.
